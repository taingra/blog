Welcome to my personal website. I am a recent computer science graduate from
Michigan Technological University. I'm interested in [[https://www.gnu.org/philosophy/free-sw.html][Free Software]], [[https://www.gnu.org/software/emacs/][GNU Emacs]],
computer security, and privacy.

This site is intentionally minimal to show that websites can be pleasant and
functional without a huge footprint. You can [[file:blog/org-mode-blog.org][read about how I built this site
here]].

Whenever possible I release my writing and source code under [[https://creativecommons.org/][Creative Commons]] or
[[http://www.gnu.org/philosophy/free-sw.html][Free Software]] licenses respectively. Majority of the content of this blog is
released under the terms of the [[https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/][Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0
International (CC BY-SA 4.0)]]. That means you can adapt my posts into new content
if you wish! So long as I am credited as the original author and you agree share
your adapted content as well by distributing it under the terms of the same
license.
