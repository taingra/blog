Welcome to my personal website.  I'm interested in [[https://www.gnu.org/philosophy/free-sw.html][Free Software]], [[https://www.gnu.org/software/emacs/][GNU
Emacs]], and analog arts.  Over the past year I've been experimenting
getting off the computer by learning to draw and other analog hobbies.

When possible I release my writing and source code under [[https://creativecommons.org/][Creative
Commons]] or [[http://www.gnu.org/philosophy/free-sw.html][Free Software]] licenses respectively.  Please check the
bottom of each webpage for licensing information.
